Victory Day

Victory Day in Bangladesh In Bangladesh, Victory Day (Bijoy Dibos) is celebrated annually on December 16 as a national holiday. It commemorates the day in 1971 when the Pakistani Armed Forces surrendered to the Allied Forces of Bangladesh and India, marking the end of the nine-month-long Bangladesh Liberation War and the establishment of the sovereign nation of Bangladesh
